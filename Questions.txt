heat rash
	- Do you live in a an area with tropical climate?
 	- [U] Are your rashes in sweat-prone areas? (e.g. groin , armpits)
	- Do you often wear tight-fitting clothing around these areas?
shingles
	- [U] Does the rash cause pain, burning sensation, numbness, tingling?
	- [U] Does it only involve a small section of your skin?
	- Does it itch?
	- Do you have a fever?
	- Do you have a headache?
	- Are you experiencing fatigue?
common rash/itch/irritation,insect bite
	- [U] Have you been bitten by, or stung by, insects such as mosquitoes, bees, or fire ants?
	- Are you experiencing fever, Vomitting/nausea? (answer should be no)
acne
	- Are the rashes in your face, forehead, chest, or upper back?
	- [U] Do you have oily skin, causing whiteheads/blackheads/pimples?
atopic dermatitis (eczema)
	- [U] Have the rashes been around for a long time? 
	- Are your rashes red and itchy?
	- Does it occur in large patches of skin?
	- Is it thickened, cracked, or scaly?
measles
	- [U] Do you have tiny white spots inside the mouth (Koplik spots)?
	- [U] Are there small raised bumps with fluid on top of red spots?
	- Do you have flat red itchy spots on your body? 
	- Do you have fever?
	- Do you have cough?
	- Are the eyes turning red?
Chickenpox
	- [U] Do you have raised red bumps?
	- Do you have fever?
	- Do you have headache?
	- Do you have flat red itchy spots on your body? 
	- Do the spots become itchy blisters?
Allergy to food or medicine
	- [U] Do you have itchy raised welts in your skin (hives)?
	- [U] Does you face, mouth, throat swell? 
	- Do you vomit?
	- Do you have itchy or watery eyes?
	- Do you have flat red itchy spots on your body? 
	- Do you have dizziness?
Dengue Rashes
	- [U] Do you bleed from the nose or gums?
	- Do you have flat red spots on your body? 
	- Do you have fever?
	- Do you vomit?
	- Do you have headache?
COVID19 Rash
	- [U] Do you have purple discoloration on fingers or toes (chilblain)?
	- [U] Do you have fishnet red-blue pattern on the skin? 
	- [U] Are you losing hair?
	- Do you have flat red itchy spots on your body? 
	- Are the eyes turning red?
